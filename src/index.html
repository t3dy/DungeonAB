⚔️ How to Play

Draft a party, then watch it delve. You set it up; the dungeon does the rest.

1 · Draft at the Table

You and three AI rivals pass packs of cards. Take one card per pack, then it passes on. Draft characters to grow your party, plus equipment, spells, and personalities to shape it. Packs always hold characters — no dead drafts.

2 · Delve the Dungeon

Before you descend, you may lay a wager on the dungeon — a condition like Monster Swarms or the Long Throne that makes the delve meaner for a bigger score. Then the party crawls room by room, deciding for itself — weighted by the personalities you drafted and the classes present. A rogue disarms traps; a cleric turns undead; an alchemist brews at lab benches. Outcomes are a spectrum, not just win or lose.

3 · Town, then Deeper

Survive and the party carries its loot and scars to town. Heal, buy potions, hire new blood from the board, or have the smith sharpen a blade — then descend to a deeper, meaner dungeon, or retire and bank your score. A wipe ends the campaign.

Watching the Delve

The Chronicle narrates every room in three beats. Toasts flag big moments — a hero falling, the boss chamber, a spell learned. Use the controls to pause, step, or change speed.
